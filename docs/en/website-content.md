# 🖼️ Website Content

A page for managing all media and content displayed on the website, including back-office sound notification settings and marketing tracking integrations.

## 📍 How to access this menu
* **Go to:** Left Menu > **"Website and Brand"** > Select **"Website Content"**

---

## ⚙️ Configuration & Features

### 1. Announcement Slides
Manage banner images for campaigns or promotions that will rotate on the website's homepage.
* **How to Add:** 
  1. Click the **"+ เพิ่มสไลด์" (Add Slide)** button at the top-right.
  2. Upload the banner image (recommended landscape aspect ratio: 16:6 or 21:6 for Desktop). You can upload a separate image for the mobile version.
  3. Specify the time range you want the banner to remain online. Leave the date fields blank if you want it to display permanently.
  4. Click save.

> ⚠️ **Banner Warning:**
> After uploading the image, **you must turn on the "แสดงบนเว็บ" (Show on Web) toggle**. If you upload it but leave the switch disabled, the banner will not appear on the website.

---

### 2. General Settings
Configure marquee text announcements and MP3 notification sounds.
* **Marquee Announcement:** Enter the announcement text. This message will scroll across the top bar of every page on the website.
* **Audio Alerts and Background Music:** Click **"เลือก MP3" (Select MP3)** to upload deposit/withdrawal warning sounds (max 5MB) or website background music (max 10MB).

> ⚠️ **Audio File Warning:**
> After selecting your MP3 files, **do not close the window or switch pages immediately**. You must scroll up and click the **"บันทึก" (Save)** button in the top-right corner of the main panel to upload and connect the audio files to the system.

---

### 3. SEO & Tracking
Optimize the website for Google Search and hook up advertising trackers.
* **SEO Meta Tags:** Set the page title and description so that Google indexes them correctly.
* **Site Verification & GTM:** Paste site verification tokens or GTM Container IDs to track user behavior.

> ⚠️ **Character Length Warning:**
> Keep titles under 60 characters and descriptions within 120-160 characters. Writing text that is too long causes Google to truncate your snippet, making it hard to read and potentially harming your SEO ranking.
