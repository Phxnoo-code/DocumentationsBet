# 👥 Online Users

A page for analyzing the number of active members on the website over time. The system records snapshots every 30 minutes to help identify peak hours and measure the success of marketing campaigns.

## 1. Overview
Displays statistical data from the snapshot recordings during the selected period:
* **Average Online:** The average number of concurrent players throughout the period.
* **Peak Online:** The highest number of online players, specifying the peak date and time.
* **Lowest Online:** The lowest number of online players, specifying the date and time.
* **Total Snapshots:** The total number of snapshots recorded for the current view.

## 2. Data Filters
Tools for filtering time periods and comparing player behavior:
* **Timeframe Selection:** Shortcut buttons for quick data selection (e.g., Today, Yesterday, 7 Days, This Month, Last Month) or custom date ranges.
* **Comparison Mode:** A toggle switch to enable comparisons, letting you overlay statistics from two different periods to analyze website growth.

## 3. Timeline Chart
* **Online User Timeline:** A line chart showing the ups and downs of active players, where each data point corresponds to a 30-minute snapshot.

## 4. Deep Insights & Patterns
Behavioral analysis based on the selected period to help plan promotions or marketing campaigns:
* **Peak Hours:** Summarizes the hours with the highest and lowest activity daily.
* **Peak Days:** Summarizes the days of the week (Monday - Sunday) with the most and least active players.
* **Hourly Pattern:** A bar chart showing the average number of users by hour (0 - 23 hrs) to identify the golden hours of the website.
* **Daily Pattern:** A bar chart showing the average number of users by day of the week to analyze weekly habits.
